What we do

What we do

Our Matchbingo gaming ecosystem enables brands to open new revenue streams from existing or new content sponsorships on TV and online. Our technology allows for real-time integration, so we can combine live TV content with gameplay on a mobile device.

We provide businesses and brands with a set of services that range from concept and design development to full technology enablement and management.

Our aim is to help brands unlock revenues and to reduce the cost of their media/sponsorship/advertising investments as well as enabling new consumer growth and retention.

Flexible commercial models for client

Our Matchbingo ecosystem is flexible and we recognise that one size does not fit every individual client’s needs. We deploy three common and proven optional models, each model offers a different investment and payment model for our clients. Every project is different, so all pricing is POA and subject to a fully agreed scope. Clients can also choose from a specific set of services such as Matchbingo Platform licencing or Matchbingo Studio services. See services

Data is at the heart of what we do, and we ensure that we are fully GDPR compliant in the processing of customer data.

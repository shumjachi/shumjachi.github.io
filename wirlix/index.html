ABOUT

The foundation of our democracy is debating and openly discussing society’s most pressing issues while understanding both sides. We’ve forgotten how to respect each other through our differences in opinion and the media is only further polarizing our society spreading hate, exaggerations and false information. Wirlix is redefining media through our invite only social platform media debate and discussion platform providing people a space to freely post opinions, exchange ideas and question everything.

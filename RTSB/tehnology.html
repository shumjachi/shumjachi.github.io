TECHNOLOGY

Our Matchbingo Platform is developed to work across multiple platforms. A centralised system architecture uses APIs to manage and synchronise data across all platforms.

System components include: Administrative controls, User Accounts, Payment gateways, Match Card Creation Algorithms, Live Score Feeds, User Notifications and Reporting.

Native iOS and Android apps as well as browser-based versions for tablets and desktops.

Hardware scalability, security, resilience, compliance, monitoring and testing is implemented and managed appropriately.

To protect our technology IP, more details are available on an NDA basis.

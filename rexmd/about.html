Men’s Health

Rex MD provides men with high-quality prescription drugs at affordable prices, with the convenience of rapid and discreet home assessment and delivery.

Men don’t often voice their health concerns, especially when it comes to things like erectile dysfunction and hair loss. That’s despite more than 50% of men dealing with hair loss, and 1 in 10 men dealing with ED.
Unfortunately, these same men are 50% less likely to go to the doctor than women, and a Cleveland Clinic survey indicated that only 3 in 5 men actually go to their annual physical.
Our silence on our health hurts our relationships, confidence, job performance, and can have more severe consequences left unaddressed. Our own machismo is taking its toll.
We set out to fix this broken culture. Healthcare doesn’t have to be embarrassing, expensive, or inconvenient. By helping men take their health into their own hands, and get proactive, we can save relationships and even save lives.

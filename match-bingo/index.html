ABOUT MATCH BINGO

Match Bingo is a new way of Playing Bingo. We have replaced the numbered balls of traditional bingo with events and scores that are generated at random during all your favourite sports matches and TV programmes.

So you can play along in real time all from the comfort of your own sofa!

We’re kicking off with “Plank Match Bingo” the unique combination of endurance planking and Bingo!

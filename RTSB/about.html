ABOUT US

We are a group of experienced media professionals and technologists. RTSB is a business built around taking the intrinsic elements of the universally popular game of Bingo and putting Bingo gameplay at the heart of popular entertainment.

Our technology enables gameplay in a live or scheduled environment, is scaleable and not constrained by territory.

We possess Operator and Supplier Licenses from the UK Gambling Commission, which enables RTSB to run a free-to-play game for cash or equivalent prizes. This allows a unique gaming experience that is not classified as gambling and therefore allows brands and content publishers to develop new revenue streams and build new customer interactions, in a manner that is responsible.
